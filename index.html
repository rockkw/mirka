About

Behind the Lens

Mirka Whitney is a visual artist and photographer whose work explores the interplay of light, shadow, and human emotion. Based in Colorado, she brings a cinematic sensibility to every frame — finding the extraordinary in quiet, unguarded moments.

Her photography spans portraiture, editorial, and landscape, each series driven by a singular question: what does it feel like to truly see? Working primarily with natural light, Mirka crafts images that linger long after you look away.
